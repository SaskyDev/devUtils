What cookies are used for

Cookies and local storage items help DevUtils remember preferences, manage consent choices, understand site usage and support monetization features where enabled.

Essential storage

  • Theme preference so the site can remember light or dark mode.
  • Cookie consent status so the site knows whether optional tracking has been accepted or rejected.

These items are used for core site behavior and preference management.

Analytics cookies

If you accept optional cookies, DevUtils may use analytics technologies such as Google Analytics to understand page visits, traffic sources, engagement patterns and tool usage.

Advertising cookies

If advertising is enabled now or in the future, DevUtils may use advertising-related cookies or signals to measure campaign performance, limit abuse and support ad delivery or monetization through services such as Google Ads.

How to manage cookies

  • Use the consent banner when it appears on first visit.
  • Use the Cookie settings button in the site footer to reopen your choice panel.
  • Clear cookies and local storage manually from your browser settings if needed.
  • Configure your browser to block or delete cookies, noting that some preferences may reset.

Policy updates

This Cookie Policy may be updated when tools, legal requirements or third-party services change. The newest version will remain available on this page.
