Privacy Policy

DevUtils is a browser-based collection of developer utilities published under the SaskyDev brand and managed by Alex Gomez Barres, based in Valencia, Spain. This policy explains what information may be collected when you use the website and how that information is handled.

Data controller

  • Project and brand: SaskyDev
  • Responsible person: Alex Gomez Barres
  • Location: Valencia, Spain
  • Contact email: agomezbarres@gmail.com

What DevUtils does

The tools on DevUtils are designed to run in your browser. In general, text, code snippets, tokens, payloads and other inputs you paste into the tools are processed client-side and are not intentionally sent to a DevUtils backend for processing.

If a specific tool ever relies on a third-party service or remote request in the future, the relevant page or this policy should be updated before that change is treated as part of normal use.

Information that may be collected

  • Basic technical information such as browser type, approximate device information, referring page and visited pages.
  • Analytics data used to understand how visitors use the site and which tools are most useful.
  • Consent preferences related to cookies and analytics.
  • Advertising-related signals if monetization features such as Google Ads are enabled and you consent where required.

DevUtils does not ask users to create accounts and does not intentionally collect sensitive personal data through normal browsing.

Analytics and advertising

DevUtils uses Google Analytics and may use advertising services such as Google Ads. These services can collect usage information, measure traffic and support site monetization.

Where applicable, optional analytics and advertising storage is controlled through the cookie consent banner. If you reject optional cookies, only essential site functionality should remain active.

Cookies and local storage

DevUtils uses cookies or similar browser storage technologies for essential preferences such as theme selection and cookie consent choices. Optional cookies may also be used for analytics and advertising if you accept them.

More detail is available in the Cookie Policy.

Third-party services

DevUtils may rely on third-party providers for analytics, advertising, hosting, performance or security. Those providers may process information according to their own privacy terms when you interact with the site.

Examples may include Google services such as Google Analytics, Google Ads and related consent or measurement tooling.

Data retention

Where DevUtils uses third-party analytics or advertising providers, retention periods are primarily determined by those providers and their configured settings. Consent choices stored locally in your browser remain until you clear them or change them through the cookie settings link in the footer.

Your choices

  • You can reject optional cookies from the consent banner.
  • You can reopen cookie settings at any time from the footer.
  • You can clear cookies or local storage from your browser settings.
  • You can stop using the site at any time if you do not agree with this policy.
  • You can contact the responsible person at agomezbarres@gmail.com for privacy-related questions.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect legal, operational or product changes. The latest version will always be published on this page with the updated revision date.
